What is The Journey?

The Journey is an event to bring business minded individuals together. The purpose is to create a community, not just connections. They say it is lonely at the top, but I say, let us bring a community together in our journey to the top! Are you ready to join in on “The Journey”?

About Me

On August 29,2023 I will be hosting an event to bring like minded business students together to connect and network with each other. The main purpose here is to not just gain a new “connection”, but to build a community of business minded individuals. As a Haskyne Student from the University of Calgary, I understand the struggles of making new and genuine friends, that is why I created this event, to solve that problem and hopefully make your post high school experience a lot better!
